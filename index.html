STAY

ROOM TYPES

DELUXE ROOM

The 360 square foot deluxe room is tastefully decorated and equipped with all the modern conveniences you'll need for a memorable stay, including plush interiors, cozy bedding, and a bathroom with toiletries. Our spacious Deluxe Rooms offer your choice of one king-size bed or two twin beds and everything you need for a productive and relaxing stay .

ROYAL DELUXE

Tastefully decorated, the 400 sq. ft. deluxe room is bedecked with your choice of one king-size bed or two twin beds with all the state-of-the-art facilities and amenities that you need to make your stay memorable – plush interiors, comfortable beddings and a bathroom with toiletries.

SEMI SUITE

This room is perfect to enjoy a cosy long weekend or a relaxing holiday with your near and dear ones. With elegant interiors, this room is quite spacious (408 sq. ft.) and furnished with a king size comfortable bed, a luxurious seating arrangement, and a bathroom equipped with all the toiletries that one might need.

PRESIDENTIAL SUITE

Our largest and most indulgent accommodations, the Presidential Suite of 2150 sq.ft offers a plush king-size bed, a separate light-filled living room, a sitting area(1223 sq. ft), and a dome (293 sq.ft). Take in sweeping views of Raipur from your perch on the fourth floor, and enjoy the convenience of a powder room in addition to a full bathroom with a Jacuzzi.

MEETING and EVENTS

PLAN YOUR MEETINGS AND EVENTS

John Donne, a poet from the 16th century, once said, "No Man Is an Island." In fact, socialising and getting together with close friends and family are essential aspects of life. We have planned a variety of meeting locations to suit all of your needs, whether they involve large-scale family gatherings, significant events and celebrations of life, exhibitions, conferences, or important business meetings. We took our cue from this desire to get together.

THE PETALS

The Ballroom Petals is located in one of the part of Vannington Court spread over 75/47 sq.ft . There is room for 200 people there. The hall, which includes a pre-function area, can be used for a variety of social gatherings, including grand weddings and receptions as well as high-profile conferences and seminars
THE CHERISH

Situated on part of the Social area, the expansive CHERISH Ballroom II (58/30 sq. ft.) and its pre-conference area is equipped to host lavish wedding functions and high-profile events such as exhibitions, conferences, and seminars. It can also accommodate as many as 100 guests. Both the CHERISH Ballrooms stand as twins in terms of space and orientation.
THE DEAL

Adjoining the corporate deals, The DEAL Banquet Hall is also suited to host as many as 10 individuals; the 19/25 sq. ft. The Deal can be booked for confidential boardroom meetings, private family get-togethers, and wine & dine meets. The Deal Banquets provide a serene vibe, which offers the idyllic ambiance to conduct private business meetings that require some serious brainstorming as well as celebrative cozy gatherings.
